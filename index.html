Overview

What makes Thread Sense useful

The app solves a real daily problem: many people know what they own but still struggle to assemble outfits quickly or remember combinations they liked before.

Why It Matters

A practical answer to decision fatigue.

Thread Sense helps users make better use of clothes they already own. Instead of starting from scratch each morning, they can build from a visual closet, reuse saved looks, and make faster outfit decisions.

Capstone Strength

A complete product story in one app.

The project combines mobile UI, image-based organization, saved data, and AI-assisted interaction. That makes it strong for a capstone because the concept is easy to understand and the implementation touches multiple technical areas.

Features

Core parts of the product

These are the main workflows your team can talk through during a class presentation or live demo.

01. Closet

Upload and categorize items.

Users add wardrobe photos from the camera or library, then organize those pieces into categories for easier browsing.

02. Picker

Build outfits from selected pieces.

The outfit picker lets users swipe through options and combine tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, jackets, and accessories.

03. Albums

Save looks for later.

Finished outfits can be saved into named albums so users can revisit, edit, and reorder their favorite combinations.

04. AI Stylist

Ask for extra outfit guidance.

The AI stylist chat adds a conversational layer where users can request ideas and attach closet photos for context.

Demo Flow

A clear way to present the app

This order keeps the story simple: define the problem, show the closet flow, build an outfit, then finish with saved looks and AI support.

01

Explain the problem.

Start with the pain point: users spend time deciding what to wear, forget good combinations, and underuse clothing they already own.

02

Show closet setup.

Demonstrate uploading clothing, assigning categories, and choosing which items should appear in the outfit picker.

03

Build and save a look.

Create an outfit in the picker and save it to an album to show how the app supports repeat use, not just one-time browsing.

04

Finish with AI support.

Open AI Stylist, attach closet photos, and show how conversational guidance makes the app feel more personal and practical.
