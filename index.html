Overview

Why ThreadSense?

Getting dressed shouldn’t feel like a creative challenge every single day. ThreadSense removes that pressure by turning your wardrobe into a system, so you can make confident outfit decisions faster and with less effort.

Why It Matters

Less stress. More consistency.

ThreadSense helps you get more out of the clothes you already own. Instead of starting from scratch each morning, you can browse your digital closet, revisit saved outfits, and quickly put together looks that work, without overthinking it.

Capstone Strength

A full-stack solution to a real problem.

This project brings together mobile design, image-based organization, persistent user data, and AI-powered suggestions. It demonstrates both technical depth and practical value.

Features

Core Parts of ThreadSense

Let's breakdown the main features in ThreadSense.

01. Closet

Upload and categorize items.

You can upload photos of your clothes directly into categories using your iPhone camera or photo library. Then, you can select up to 10 items per category of clothing to be shown in Outfit Picker, so you don't have to swipe through your whole closet each time.

02. Outfit Picker

Manually build outfits from your selected pieces.

Swipe through your clothes to combine tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, jackets, and accessories.

03. Saved Outfits

Save looks for later.

Finished outfits can be saved into named albums, so you can revisit, edit, and reorder your favorite combinations.

04. AI Stylist

Ask for outfit suggestions.

Chat with the AI Stylist where you can request ideas for new looks, attach a photo from your closet for context, and save any generated outfits you love into an album.

Problem Statement

But what should I wear?

Choosing what to wear can be more time-consuming than it should be. Whether it’s getting ready for a typical day or preparing for a special event, people often spend extra time trying on outfits, second-guessing their choices, or falling back on the same looks. This process can lead to frustration, clutter, and overlooked clothing items that rarely get used. Even on a daily basis, outfit decisions add up—turning a simple task into a repeated source of decision fatigue.

Our target audience is teens to young adults that want a refreshed view of their wardrobe or a new look for an upcoming event. However, ThreadSense is open to all ages and there for all the moments in life!
Solution Summary

How does ThreadSense solve that problem?

ThreadSense simplifies the outfit planning process by turning a user’s wardrobe into an accessible digital closet. Users can upload and organize their clothing, browse items without physically taking them out, and build outfits from anywhere. The app also allows users to save their favorite looks for quick reference, reducing the need to start from scratch each time. In addition, the AI Stylist provides personalized outfit suggestions based on the user’s own clothing, helping them discover new combinations and make better use of items they might otherwise overlook.

The core workflow is simple: upload your clothes, explore your digital closet, create outfits, and save your favorites. Unlike doing this manually, ThreadSense keeps everything in one place and adds AI-powered suggestions, making outfit planning faster, easier, and more creative.
